## SECUR-EU — Enhancing Security of European SMEs

**SECUR-EU** is an EU-funded project focused on strengthening the cybersecurity posture of **Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)** and supporting resilience in **critical infrastructures**. The project started on **1 December 2023** and brings together **16 consortium partners** across Europe.

## Mission

SECUR-EU aims to:
- Foster a shared **cybersecurity awareness culture** for SMEs
- Enhance and promote **open-source security solutions** suitable for SME adoption
- Disseminate **ethical hacking / white-hat testing practices** through the **HackOlympics** initiative
- Advance **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)** using **state-of-the-art technologies**, including **AI**

## Key activities

- **Stakeholder Group:** SMEs are invited to participate and provide input during implementation.
- **Training Sessions:** Dedicated trainings on cybersecurity awareness and solutions for SMEs.
- **HackOlympics:** Testing and validation of SECUR-EU outcomes by ethical hackers in controlled environments.

## Events & timeline (high level)

- **Kick-off (Dec 1, 2023):** Hybrid meeting in Predeal, Romania  
- **Stakeholder engagement (Q1 2024)**
- **Training sessions (Q2–Q3 2024)**
- **HackOlympics (Q4 2024)**

## Funding

SECUR-EU is supported by the European Union’s **DIGITAL** programme under **Grant Agreement No. 101128029**.
